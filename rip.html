On April 27th, 2018, we lost our dear friend, Nicholas Nguyen.

He fought bravely against the terrorists in Afghanistan that threatened freedom, liberty, and democracy. He gave his life to defend the beautiful United States of America, and his sacrifice will be remembered and honored.

Nicholas was a devout Christian. Every Sunday, bright and early, he was in church. He offered his services to the church and to God. He always carried a Bible on him. Even when he was in Afghanistan, fighting against those that hate God and America, he was praying everyday.

May he rest in peace. I know that someday, we shall all join him and God up in Heaven, and we shall celebrate an eternity in paradise.
