Meet the Team

From left to right: Daniel, Jordan, Nathan, Nicholas (RIP), and Diego

What's up guys, and welcome to Nathaniel Hilarious! If you wanted to learn more about the people behind this channel, then you've come to the right place! We're a small group of devoted creators, dedicated to bringing you the best content possible! I know what you might be thinking, "How could they possibly make such good content at such a fast rate?" Well, I'll let you in on a little secret.

We use ChatGPT and various other AI services to not only give us ideas for our videos, but also write the script for them too! Once we're able to get the content creation done by AI (like our king Kwebbelkop), we'll be a nearly 100% AI driven channel! The entire team works tirelessly writing dozens and dozens of prompts for the AI. It's a very meticulous process, requiring sweat, blood, and tears to get the show rolling. Don't be fooled, writing prompts is a very difficult task! Not just anybody can do it!
